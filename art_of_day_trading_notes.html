Art of day trading reading notes

Written by Keith McDonnell. Last updated on Friday, September 09, 2011.

= Questions

= Action points
Find the top 50 ETF’s with average (high – low)/opening > 1.75%

= Notes

5 rules for developing a day trading system:

  • Long test periods, eg 10 years
  • Wide range of variable values generate good results, otherwise you’re probably
  • optimising for the noise
  • Limited number of rules
  • Real time & out-of-sample testing
  • Walk-forward testing
  • look for the market that has the largest price range; it should be the best candidate for day-trading
